What Is Adar?

Adar is an online marketplace that connects people who want to rent out their homes with people who are looking for accommodations in specific locales.
The idea behind Adar is simple: Find a way for local people to make some extra money renting out their spare home or room to people visiting the area. Hosts using this platform get to advertise their rentals to millions of people worldwide, with the reassurance that a big company will handle payments and offer support when needed.
For guests, Adar can offer a homey place to stay that has more character, perhaps even with a kitchen to avoid dining out, often at a lower price than what hotels charge.
